Joseph Lagerman is serving 32.5 years for a crime that harmed no one. He was never given a fair trial, and the system failed him at every turn. Help us fight for his freedom.

Key Facts

  • No prior felony convictions at the time of arrest or sentencing
  • No one was injured—not even a scratch reported
  • No civilian witnesses testified in any hearing or trial
  • Sentenced to 32.5 years, just below the statutory maximum
  • Judge displayed visible bias, including inappropriate courtroom decor and commentary

The Story They Tried to Bury

Joseph Lagerman never got a trial. Not one civilian witness ever showed up. No opportunity to cross-examine. No second chances. The only thing the system gave him was time—32.5 years of it.

He was sentenced not just for what happened that day, but for everything the state could dig up and lump in. There were no other open cases. No violent record. But you wouldn’t know that by the way they came down on him.

And in the courtroom—where justice should live—Joseph stood in front of a judge who never looked him in the eyes. The same judge who displayed bobbleheads of Yoda and Darth Vader during sentencing...

But Joey’s not done. And neither are we. Truth doesn’t die in silence—it rises with every voice that dares to speak it.
